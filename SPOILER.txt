           SPOILERS FOR THE LEVELS (in case you can't figure them out)


=====================================================================================



SPOILERS FOR LEVEL -69:
There's an invisible area you have to avoid. Then, once you're on the wooden platform
you need to only use 1 jump to get the the final platform.





SPOILERS FOR LEVEL -420:
You need to fall out of the level




SPOILERS FOR LEVEL -69420:
You need to crush yourself between the moving platform and the wall. The timing is a
little tricky, and you need to be right next to the wall for it to count.






SPOILERS FOR LEVEL aHM72p`:
You need to try and crush yourself underneath the top left door
